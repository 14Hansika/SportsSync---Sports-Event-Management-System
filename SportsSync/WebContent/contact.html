Frequently Asked Questions

You can register as a player by clicking on the "Register" button in the navigation menu and filling out the player registration form with your basic details.

To organize matches, you need to register as an organizer. During registration, select the "Organizer" role to access match creation and management features.

Players can cancel their participation through their dashboard, while organizers can cancel entire matches through the organizer panel. Please refer to our cancellation policy for any applicable fees.

We currently support cricket, football, badminton, basketball, tennis, volleyball, and several other popular sports. New sports are added regularly based on user demand.
